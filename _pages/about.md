I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electronics at the 
[University of Laghouat](https://www.lagh-univ.dz/en/), Algeria, where I 
teach and conduct research in control engineering and dynamical systems.

## Research Interests
My research focuses on the analysis and control of complex dynamical systems, including:
- **Nonlinear control systems** — stability analysis, Lyapunov methods, feedback linearization
- **Hybrid dynamical systems** — modeling and control of switching systems
- **Power electronics** — multilevel DC-DC converters, sliding mode control
- **Energy systems** — fuel cell systems, MPPT control for renewable energy

## Teaching
I teach undergraduate and graduate courses including:
- Analysis and Control of Nonlinear Systems
- Linear Systems
- Digital and Analog Control
- Introduction to Programming with MATLAB
